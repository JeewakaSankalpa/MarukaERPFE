# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Maruka ERP is used by administrators, managers, finance staff, project teams, HR staff, and store personnel. Users work inside a shared operational system and need to understand current work, record transactions, and resolve exceptions without searching across multiple modules.

## Product Purpose

The product connects finance, inventory, procurement, projects, people, customers, and suppliers in one ERP. Success means operational records stay trustworthy, urgent work is visible, and each role can move from a summary to the underlying transaction or project quickly.

## Brand Personality

Clear, dependable, practical. The interface should feel calm during routine work and direct when something needs attention.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative analytics, hidden critical details, novelty navigation, excessive animation, low-contrast status colors, and dashboards that require hovering or opening every card to discover an issue.

## Design Principles

1. Put exceptions before activity.
2. Show the amount, owner, project, and due date needed to act.
3. Keep summaries traceable to the underlying operational screen.
4. Use consistent status language and severity across modules.
5. Prefer dense, readable information over decorative space.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, semantic status labels that do not rely on color alone, responsive layouts, and reduced-motion support.
